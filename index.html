Hi. Thanks for visiting my site!

I'm a Toronto-area web developer, with a background in broadcast media and written communications. After almost entering web development around 2000, I chose to go into TV/Radio production. I loved it, but found I was being drawn into administrative positions.

I left the industry and tried my hand a few different things, including writing fiction for about 5 years. This is tough industry to earn a living from, but I loved writing.

In the fall of 2015 I took a web development bootcamp at BrainStation, here in Toronto. I loved it. Since finishing that, I've been working as a freelance web developer, working mainly with a designer building custom themes in WordPress. From me, you'll get a well-rounded person, who is technically inclined and also creatively inspired.
